About Us

Afro-Portuguese chain restaurant serving flame-grilled chicken in spicy chilli sauce.
We are an international casual dining restaurant chain originating from South Africa, with a Mozambican/Portuguese theme.
Founded in 1987, Nandoso operates about 1,000 outlets in 30 countries.
Nando's specializes in chicken dishes with lemon and herb, mango and lime, medium, hot, extra hot or extra extra hot peri-peri marinades
